# LC Chemistry — Higher Level Notes

A revision website for Leaving Certificate Higher Level Chemistry. Topic-by-topic notes, definitions, diagrams, quick-reference tables and exam-style practice questions covering the full syllabus.

> Built for 5th/6th Year students · 2024/2025

---

## Topics covered

### Atomic Theory (1–10)
Periodic Table · Formulas · History of the Atom · Radioactivity · Bohr, Spectra & Configurations · Mass Spectrometer · Ionisation Energy · Radius, Electronegativity & Ionic Bonding · Covalent, Sigma/Pi & Dative Bonds · Intermolecular Forces

### Organic Chemistry (1–10)
Alkanes, Alkenes & Alkynes · Crude Oil, Ethene & Ethyne · Chloroalkanes & Alcohols · Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids & Esters · Ionic Addition, Free Radical Substitution & Polymerisation · Benzene & Clove Oil · Elimination, Redox & Synthesis · Esterification, Hydrolysis & Soap · Chromatography, Instruments & Recrystallisation · Preparation of Benzoic Acid

### Stoichiometry (1–4)
The Mole & Avogadro · Empirical & Molecular Formula · Percentage Composition · Balanced Equations, Yield & Limiting Reagent

### Other core topics
Acids & Bases · Anion Tests · Chemical Equilibrium · Definitions Glossary · Electrochemistry · Gas Laws · Oxidation & Reduction · pH & Indicators · Rates of Reaction · Shapes of Molecules · Thermochemistry · Volumetric Analysis · Water

---

## Features

- **Three switchable themes** — Classic, Editorial, Study
- **Collapsible sidebar** navigation across all pages
- **Scroll-reveal animations** and a reading progress bar
- **Hover interactions** on cards, reaction boxes and nav links
- **Floating back-to-top button**
- **Smooth colour transitions** when switching themes
- **Mobile-friendly** — nav links auto-scroll to content on small screens
- **SVG support diagrams** for key experiments and concepts
- **Expandable answers** on practice questions
- **Colour-coded callout boxes** for definitions, notes, exam tips and results
